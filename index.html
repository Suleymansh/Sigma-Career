Who we are

Who we are

Sigma Software is a multinational company that provides our customers with best-in-class solutions. We are empowered by mix of cultures, approaches and views. We take the best from every nation to eventually create superior software.

Founded in 2002, ever since we were leaded forward by dedication and flexibility, striving for perfect quality of services and willingness to help our clients bring their businesses to the new levels. Since its inception Sigma Software was intended to become the best place for IT specialists to fulfill their ideas and potential working on the most interesting and complex solutions and engaging cutting-edge technologies.

 

We offer remote job opportunities around the globe! We are empowered by mix of cultures, approaches and views. We take the best from every nation to eventually create superior software. Our clients are world known companies as AstraZeneca, Scania, SAS, Fortum, DanAds, Viaplay, and others.

Join us in Poland, Hungary, Ukraine, Portugal, Czech Republic, Latin America, or remotely from any corner of the world.
With Sigma Software you get flexible schedule, culture of professional growth and development, strong international team, world-known customers working in 10+ business domains, variety of technologies and responsible employer.
